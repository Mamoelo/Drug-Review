About Limko Medical

An AI-powered health companion that analyzes your symptoms and recommends the most effective medications based on real patient data.

What is Limko Medical?

Limko Medical is an AI-powered system that analyzes patient drug reviews to predict medical conditions and recommend effective medications. It uses machine learning models trained on over 18,000 real patient reviews to provide fast, data-driven guidance on common conditions.

How It Works

1

Natural Language Processing: Your symptom description is analyzed using NLP techniques including TF-IDF vectorization and VADER sentiment analysis.

2

Machine Learning Classification: An ensemble of models (scikit-learn + XGBoost) predicts the most likely condition with confidence scoring.

3

Recommendation Engine: Based on historical patient reviews, the most effective medications are ranked and presented with effectiveness ratings.
