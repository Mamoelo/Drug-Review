About Drug Advisor

What is Drug Advisor?

Drug Advisor is an AI-powered system that analyzes patient drug reviews to predict medical conditions and recommend effective medications. It uses machine learning models trained on over 18,000 real patient reviews.

How It Works
  1. Natural Language Processing: Your symptom description is analyzed using NLP techniques.
  2. Machine Learning Classification: An ensemble of models predicts the most likely condition.
  3. Recommendation Engine: Based on historical patient reviews, effective medications are suggested.
Model Performance
  • Accuracy: 94.0%
  • Precision: 94.1%
  • Recall: 93.8%
  • F1 Score: 93.7%
Supported Conditions
  • Depression
  • High Blood Pressure (Hypertension)
  • Type 2 Diabetes
Disclaimer
Medical Disclaimer: Drug Advisor is for informational purposes only and does not constitute medical advice. Always consult with a qualified healthcare provider before making any medical decisions or starting any treatment.
Technology Stack
  • Backend: Flask (Python)
  • Machine Learning: scikit-learn, XGBoost
  • NLP: TF-IDF, VADER Sentiment
  • Frontend: Bootstrap 5, Chart.js
